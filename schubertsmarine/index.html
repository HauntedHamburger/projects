Professional Marine Services

Schubert’s Marine is the facility to go to for all recreational, sport fish, and sailing services. From 10” to 100”, we serve a large community of boaters and look forward to helping you get back on the water.

What We Do

Services We Commonly Perform

Bottom Work

Prevent unnecessary water damage and costly fiberglass blister repairs with regular resurfacing and painting. We prepare each surface using disc and orbital sanders for maximum adhesion of 2mil rolled on marine paints.

Get a Quick Estimate Here

Mechanical Repair

Regular preventative maintanence will keep you off the blocks, Schubert's Marine provides customers with routine care for inboard and outboard power systems, while offering full scope of services for repairs and replacements.

Electrical

Float switches to radars, speakers to flood lights, we provide solutions to all aspects of maintaining the safety of your investment and improving the quality of your on water experience with lighting and digital upgrades and installation.

Haul Outs

Utilizing our 90 ft. Sea King crane, up to 65 tons, we conduct hoisted surveys, inspections, routine algae removal via pressure washing, launching and trailering for long haul including mast removal for sail.
